How to reach the success screen

The green success screen only appears after a successful test payment. If you see “International cards are not supported”, the card number is not valid for INR test mode.

  • Fastest: UPI → enter success@razorpay
  • Netbanking / Wallet: pick any option → click Success on the mock bank page
  • Indian test card: 5267 3181 8797 5449 (any future expiry, any CVV, OTP 1234)

Do not use 4111 1111 1111 1111 here — Razorpay treats it as international for INR.
